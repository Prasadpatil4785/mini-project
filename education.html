Farmer Education Hub

Soil Preparation: Test soil pH and nutrient levels. Use organic matter to improve soil structure and fertility.

Organic Fertilizers: Apply compost, manure, and bio-fertilizers to enhance soil health without chemicals.

Water Management: Implement rainwater harvesting and efficient irrigation to conserve water.

Seed Selection: Choose high-yield, disease-resistant varieties suited to local climate.

Disease Handling: Monitor crops regularly, use integrated pest management, and apply natural remedies.

Drip Irrigation: Delivers water directly to roots, reducing waste and improving efficiency.

Hydroponics: Grow plants without soil using nutrient-rich water solutions for faster growth.

Smart Farming using AI: Use sensors and AI to monitor crops, predict yields, and optimize resources.

Drone Spraying: Precision application of pesticides and fertilizers using drones for better coverage.

Loan Guidance: Understand different loan types, interest rates, and repayment terms.

Subsidies: Learn about government subsidies for seeds, fertilizers, and equipment.

Insurance: Crop insurance covers losses due to natural disasters or pests.

Government Schemes: Explore schemes like PM-KISAN for direct income support.
