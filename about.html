Shivaji Maharaj’s Farmer Policies

No Tax During Drought

Shivaji Maharaj implemented policies to waive taxes during droughts to protect farmers.

Fair Tax System (पैठणी व्यवस्था)

A system where taxes were collected fairly, ensuring farmers' welfare.

Protection from Mughals & Sardars

Protected farmers from external threats and exploitation.

Stored Grain for Bad Seasons

Maintained grain reserves for distribution during famines.

Encouraged Irrigation

Promoted irrigation projects to improve agricultural productivity.

Anti-Exploitation Policies

Implemented measures to prevent exploitation by middlemen.

Direct Communication with Ryots

Established direct channels for farmers to voice concerns.

Quick Problem-Solving Governance

Efficient administration to resolve issues promptly.
